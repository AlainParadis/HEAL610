Employability in higher education: a review of key stakeholders’ perspectives

Key: cheng_employability_2022

Author(s): Cheng, Ming and Adekola, Olalekan and Albia, JoClarisse and Cai, Sanfa

Year: 2022

Abstract: Purpose – Employability is a key concept in higher education. Graduate employment rate is often used to assess the quality of university provision, despite that employability and employment are two different concepts. This paper will increase the understandings of graduate employability through interpreting its meaning and whose responsibility for graduate employability from the perspectives of four key stakeholders: higher education institutions, students, government and employers.
